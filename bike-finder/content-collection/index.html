Buying Guide Labels

  • Road Bikes
    • great for fitness biking, commutes or casual racing
    • lightweight and aerodynamic
    • narrow tires for smooth ride on paved terrain
  • Hybrid Bikes
    • all-purpose bikes for roads, trails, and moderate inclines
    • lightweight but can handle rugged terrain
    • multiple gears and wide tires for riding in varied terrains
  • Mountain Bikes
    • great for off-road biking, trails, steep paths, and rough terrain
    • sturdy frame, firm brakes, and suspension to soften impacts
    • low gears and wide tires for uphill climbs
  • Electric Bikes
    • electric motor to help you in those pesky inclines and enhance your riding
    • rechargable battery
  • Specialty Bikes
    • looking for BMX, vintage styles, and other adult bike styles?
